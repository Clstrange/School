Feather Gate Ranch-Service

How we breed our gait

With the benefit of using easy-going stallions, we mainly breed our own mares in-hand. The mares are lightly worked during gestation, and given necessary minerals, supplements, and fresh feed.

Foals are imprinted upon birsth, and allowed a few days to adjust and bond with their dams Halter-training is done gradually, usually through their first year, using gentle R+ methods. As a result of that, they become well-behaved youngsters that like to shove their noeses right into the halter!

Not every weaning, yearling, etc. is sold with perfect manners, but they are a perfect template for our own training style to step in and get to work.
