Queue: FIF structure: First In, First Out

	Queue(): Create the queue, leaving it empty
	isEmpty(): Test whether the queue is empty or not
	enqueue(item): Adds a new item to the rear of the queue(if it is not full)
	dequeue(): The item from the front of the queue will be removed and returns the item, if not empty
	returive()L Retirives the front element of queue, if not empty
